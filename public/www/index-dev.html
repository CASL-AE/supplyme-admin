The VeriDoc Software

VeriDoc provides intelligent automation and software services to small and emerging homeshare managers that are looking for opportunities to increase revenue and maximize efficiency through automation.

VeriDoc uses a propritary algorithm developed over 5 years. VeriDoc also allows your owners to log in and run reports, giving you the freedom to manage other aspects of the business.

What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to grow your business

Dynamic Algorithm

Because we know that every property manager is different, we allow the manager to customize thier algorithm settings to meet their needs.

Automated Updates

The manager sets how often the algorithm needs to run, and the software does the rest.

Owner Reporting

By using VeriDoc, managers can give thier owners the access they need. Owners can login and view reports.

Cryptocurrency

By using VeriDoc, guests can create or import a crypto wallet and add funds using coinbase or traditional methods. Once they have funds in their “cash” wallet, they can proceed with their crypto payment method. More information available.

Financial Management

Currently VeriDoc integrates into quickbooks by recording daily transfers from VeriDoc to the Establishment, as well as each transaction in VeriDoc. The web portal also has access to enterprise level reports that establishments can generate to accurately predict buying patterns and manage inventory.

Occupancy Tracking

By using the real time occupancy feature, the price is reduced to encourage additional guests or the price is increased to assume a maximum gain of revenue. The price changes based on machine learning and perimeters set during the initiation of the application.
