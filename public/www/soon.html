What is Xupply?

Xupply is an app that efficiently connects and manages the ENTIRE logistical transaction between demand and supply.

More specifically, it allows people in need, such as, but not limited to healthcare professionals (HCP’s) to make requests for medical supplies and equipment, for manufacturers and volunteers to offer to fulfill these demands, for financiers to fund the transaction, and for industry leaders to handle logistics. #COVID19
